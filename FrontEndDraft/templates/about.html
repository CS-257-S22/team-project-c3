About

What2Eat is a website devoted to helping users find products from a given brand. What2Eat gathers it's data from the USDA food information database. The database was created in April 2022, and contains all of the USDA certfied prodcuts for individual brands. In our project, we use an arbitrarily taken section of the data set. The products that you see are real products but the range of them are not representative of all the products in the individual data set. Contributers to this project include Morgan Graves, Alice Cutter.
